## When to Use Each Template

- **Template 1:** Cold outreach, no prior knowledge of their ads
- **Template 2:** They're actively running ads (check FB Ad Library / TikTok)
- **Template 3:** After you have 1-2 successful case studies
- **Template 4:** When you can see their specific ads and provide insights
- **Template 5:** LinkedIn when you can't find email

## Email Tracking Tools

- **Streak (Gmail):** See if email was opened
- **Hunter.io:** Verify email deliverability
- **Mixmax:** Schedule follow-ups automatically
- **GMass:** Send personalized mass emails

---

## Success Metrics

- **Open Rate:** Aim for 40%+ (good subject lines)
- **Response Rate:** Aim for 10%+ (10 emails = 1 response)
- **Conversion to Call:** Aim for 50% (1 response = 0.5 calls booked)

**Expected:**
- Send 20 emails ‚Üí 2 responses ‚Üí 1 call ‚Üí 0.3 clients
- **To get 2-3 pilot clients: Send ~50-60 emails**
