# How to Find Micro-Influencers Manually (2-3 hours)

## Quick TikTok Search Method

**Language Learning Niche:**
```

#fitnessmotivation (15.1B views)
```

**How to search:**
1. Open TikTok app or web
2. Click Search ‚Üí Enter hashtag
3. Filter: "Accounts" (not videos)
4. Look for profiles with 10k-100k followers

---

### Step 2: Filter Criteria

**Good Influencer Profile:**
‚úÖ **10k-100k followers** (micro-influencer sweet spot)
‚úÖ **Bio mentions email** or "DM for collabs" or link in bio
‚úÖ **Posted in last 7 days** (active)
‚úÖ **Engagement rate > 3%** (check likes/views ratio)
‚úÖ **Authentic content** (not bot engagement)
‚úÖ **English speaking** or your target market

**Red Flags:**
‚ùå No posts in 30+ days
‚ùå Followers but low views (bought followers)
‚ùå Spammy content
‚ùå No contact info anywhere

---

### Step 3: Extract Contact Info (15 min)

**Where to find email:**
1. **TikTok Bio** - often has email directly
2. **Link in Bio** - click and check website for contact
3. **Instagram** (if linked) - often easier to find email there
4. **Comments** - sometimes they respond with email

**Tools to help:**
- **Hunter.io** - Find email by name/domain (FREE 25 searches/month)
- **RocketReach** - Social profile ‚Üí email lookup

## Advanced: Search by Similar Accounts

### Method 1: TikTok "Similar Accounts"

1. Find ONE good influencer in your niche
2. Go to their profile
3. Scroll down to "Similar Accounts"
4. TikTok suggests similar creators!

### Method 2: Check Their Following

1. Go to good influencer profile
2. Click "Following"
3. Often follows other creators in same niche
4. Find more micro-influencers there

### Method 3: Video Comments

1. Watch popular video in your niche
2. Read comments
3. Click on commenters with lots of likes
4. Many are micro-influencers themselves!

## Time-Saving Tips

### 1. Use TikTok Creator Marketplace (if accessible)
- TikTok's official influencer database
- Free if you have Business account
- Already verified creators

### 2. Check Instagram Cross-Posts
- Many TikTokers also on Instagram
- Easier to DM on Instagram
- Often have email in Instagram bio

### 3. Look for "Collab" Keywords
- Bio mentions: "Collabs: DM" or "Brand deals: email@"
- These creators are ALREADY looking for partnerships!

## What To Do With The List

Once you have 20-30 influencers:

1. **Import to your platform:**
   ```
   POST /api/v1/influencers/import-csv
   Upload the spreadsheet
   ```

2. **Segment by quality:**
   - A-tier: Perfect fit, has email, high ER
   - B-tier: Good fit, need to find email
   - C-tier: Maybe, lower priority

3. **Start outreach:**
   - Email A-tier first
   - DM B-tier on TikTok/Instagram
   - Keep C-tier as backup
