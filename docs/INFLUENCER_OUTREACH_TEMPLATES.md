## Response Rate Expectations

**Typical results:**
- Email open rate: 30-50%
- Response rate: 10-20%
- Acceptance rate: 5-10%

**To get 10 influencers to say yes:**
- Send 100-200 outreach emails
- Expect 20-40 responses
- 10-20 will be interested
- 10 will actually deliver

**Pro tip:** Over-recruit by 2x to account for flakes.

## Legal Note

Always include:
- Clear usage rights (e.g., "30 days for paid ads")
- FTC disclosure requirement ("Must include #ad or #sponsored")
- Content ownership (they can keep on their page)

Sample contract clause:
```
By accepting payment, you grant [Company] rights to use the video
in paid advertising for 30 days. Video remains on your profile.
All posts must include #ad disclosure per FTC guidelines.
```
